Deep-linking and highlighting

Try tapping the Shift key twice. Paragraph symbols (¶) should appear next to each paragraph in the text above. Click that symbol to get a link to that individual paragraph and click the sentences within it to highlight text. You can now copy the URL (web address) from your address bar to use and share these links, and others will be able to see which sections you have highlighted.

Made possible with the New York Times’ tool Emphasis.
